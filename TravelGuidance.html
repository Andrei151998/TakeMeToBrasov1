Travel Guidance

Planning a visit to Brașov? Here are some essential tips to help you get the most out of your trip:

  • Getting There: Brașov is about 2.5 hours by train or car from Bucharest. Trains run frequently and are reliable(OpenAI, 2025).
  • Getting Around: Public transportation is affordable and easy to use. Taxis and rideshare services are also available(OpenAI, 2025).
  • Currency: The official currency is the Romanian Leu (RON). Credit/debit cards are widely accepted, but cash is useful in smaller shops or rural areas(OpenAI, 2025).
  • Language: Romanian is the official language, but many people in tourist areas speak English well(OpenAI, 2025).
  • Best Time to Visit: Spring and autumn are ideal for sightseeing, while winter is perfect for skiing in Poiana Brașov(OpenAI, 2025).
  • Safety: Brașov is very safe for tourists. Just keep an eye on your belongings in busy areas, as with any city(OpenAI, 2025).
  • Electricity: Romania uses the European standard 230V plug (Type C & F). Bring an adapter if you're coming from the UK or North America(OpenAI, 2025).
